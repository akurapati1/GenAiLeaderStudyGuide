## 💡 How to Use This Guide

1. **Clone or download** this repo
2. **Open the HTML file** in your browser
3. **Work through the tabs** in order — Domains → Topics → Tools → Concepts
4. **Use the Study Plan tab** to structure your weeks
5. **Drill the Exam Tips tab** the week before your exam
6. Supplement with the [official Google exam guide](https://cloud.google.com/learn/certification/generative-ai-leader) and free labs on [Google Cloud Skills Boost](https://cloudskillsboost.google)

## 🔑 Top 5 Things to Know for the Exam

1. **Google AI Studio vs. Vertex AI Studio** — Prototype quickly vs. enterprise production. This distinction appears constantly.
2. **RAG pipeline** — How retrieval-augmented generation works end-to-end and when to use it over fine-tuning.
3. **Temperature parameter** — Low = deterministic/factual, High = creative/varied. Know when to adjust.
4. **Google's Responsible AI principles** — All 7. Especially fairness, transparency, and accountability in scenario questions.
5. **Tool → Scenario matching** — Document AI ≠ Vision API. Agentspace ≠ NotebookLM. Know every tool's exact use case.
